        Definition: Blogging is the act of creating and publishing content online in 
         the form of articles (blog posts) on a website.  
        Purpose: Blogs can be personal, educational, business-related, or niche-focused,
         covering various topics like travel, tech, food, or finance.  
        Platform: Popular blogging platforms include WordPress, Blogger, Medium, and Wix.  
        Monetization: Blogs can generate income through ads, affiliate marketing, 
         sponsored posts, and product sales.  
        SEO & Engagement: Effective blogging involves search engine optimization (SEO) 
         and audience interaction through comments and shares.
